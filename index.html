IGOR IS AOTY

By: Gabe M.

IGOR, a most anticipated album by tyler the creator, released on May 17th 2019
was a totally new project for the artist that allowed him to get his musical creativity to the max, even though he
was worried how the album would be received. It is a very experimental record, with a mix of 80's punk and RnB soul with a lot of synths
as it is Tylers' trademark.
